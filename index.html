Information & Help

Alert Color System

NWS Warnings (Very Dark Red)

Genuine weather warnings from the National Weather Service. These appear in very dark red to distinguish them from manually scheduled alerts.

Manual Warnings (Standard Red)

Manually advanced or scheduled weather alerts. These use the standard red color to indicate they are not from the NWS.

Weather Watches (Orange)

Weather conditions are favorable for severe weather. Monitor conditions closely.

Weather Advisories (Yellow)

Less severe weather conditions that may cause inconvenience or minor damage.

No Active Alerts (Green)

No weather alerts currently active for this location.

Filtering System

Site Type Filters

Filter locations by their site type (warehouse, plant, office, etc.). When a filter is turned off, markers remain on the map but appear in light grey.

Alert Level Filters

Filter alerts by severity level (warning, watch, advisory, none). Filtered alerts show as grey markers on the map but remain visible.

Update Schedule

Automatic Updates

Weather data refreshes automatically every 2 minutes from the National Weather Service API.

Manual Refresh

Click the sync button in the map controls to manually refresh all weather data immediately.

Map Features

Interactive Markers

Click on any map marker to see detailed location information and current weather conditions.

Alert Polygons

Weather alert zones are displayed as colored polygons on the map when available from the NWS.

Pulsing Animation

Severe weather warnings and watches have a pulsing animation to draw attention.
